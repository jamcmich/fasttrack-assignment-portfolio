About Me

My name is Jacob McMichael and I have been studying computer programming since 2016 when I began college. I want to create meaningful experiences through design and study the social impact of technology on our everyday lives.

As developers and creative thinkers we need to keep an open mind and adopt an adaptable, inventive learning scheme to keep up with the world. This includes learning and applying the principles of human centered design.

My projects always begin with identifying the client's problems and finding suitable solutions. I work directly with users and shareholders to develop meaningful applications.
